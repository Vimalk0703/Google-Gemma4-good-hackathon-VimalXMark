## §11 — WHAT THIS MEANS  (Impact arithmetic)

| Claim | Math | Source for inputs |
|-------|------|-------------------|
| **~175,000 children/year if Malaika hits the 15% IMCI number on pneumonia + diarrhea.** | 0.15 × 1,170,000 = 175,500 | UNICEF 1.17M figure × Cochrane 15% reduction. Both cited above. |
| **~480 children/day.** | 175,500 / 365 ≈ 481 | Same as above. |

> *We are explicit that this is a* **projection**, *not a measured outcome.* The 15% is what IMCI achieved in trials of human-delivered IMCI. We claim Malaika *could* reach somewhere near that number; we have not proven it. The script's wording — *"if Malaika gets us anywhere close to that fifteen-percent IMCI number"* — preserves this conservatism.
